WATCH: BLUE IVY DANCES ONSTAGE DURING MOM BEYONCES RENAISSANCE CONCERT

Beyoncé’s daughter, Blue Ivy Carter, surprised fans during her mother’s “Renaissance Tour” concert in Paris on May 26. In video clips shared online, Blue showcased her best dance moves to songs “My Power” and “Black Parade”, capturing the hearts of the audience.

Sporting a glittering mock-neck top and silver cargo pants, Blue looked every bit the superstar as she donned a pair of thin oval sunglasses and danced alongside Beyoncé’s backup dancers. Beyoncé proudly acknowledged her daughter’s presence, proclaiming, “Give it up for Blue,” prompting the crowd to erupt in applause and cheers.

This wasn’t the first time Blue performed with her mom onstage. Earlier in the year, she performed a duet of “Brown Skin Girl” at the Atlantis The Royal Resort in Dubai, showcasing her exceptional talent at such a young age.

While Blue Ivy stole the spotlight on stage, other notable figures were also present at the concert. Megan Thee Stallion, accompanied by Blue Ivy’s father and Roc Nation boss, JAY-Z, could be seen among the excited crowd. Blue Ivy’s grandmother, Tina Knowles, attended the concert as well and took to Instagram to express her immense pride and joy. She shared a heartfelt post exclaiming, “Last night, I saw my beautiful grandbaby (11) dance in front of almost 70 thousand people! She killed it and was as cool as a cucumber! Blue Ivy Carter ❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️ No Fear❤️❤️.”

The “Renaissance Tour” will continue to captivate audiences as it makes its way through the UK and Europe, with the highly anticipated North American leg scheduled to commence in July. Fans eagerly await more exceptional performances from Beyoncé and, perhaps, additional surprise appearances by the talented Blue Ivy Carter.
