MS JACKY OH, THE MOTHER OF DC YOUNG FLYS KIDS, HAS PASSED AWAY

Ms Jacky Oh!, the longtime partner of DC Young Fly and mother to their kids, has tragically passed away. According to TMZ, Jacky died in Miami, although the specific details surrounding her death are still unknown. A now-deleted social media post mentioned that she was in Miami for a “mommy makeover.”

In the post, she wrote, “Getting ready for my mommy makeover with Dr. Zach!! Stay tuned to my … i do a lot . but being a mommy is my favorite.”

Known by her real name, Jaclyn Smith, Ms Jacky Oh! and DC Young Fly first met in 2015 when he made his debut on MTV’s popular show, “Wild ‘N Out.” Although they never married, their love endured, resulting in the birth of three beautiful children. They welcomed their daughters, Nova and Nala, and just last year, they were blessed with the arrival of their son, Prince.

The devastating news of Ms Jacky Oh!’s passing has left family, friends, and fans in shock. At only 32 years old, her life was tragically cut short, leaving behind a void that will be felt by many.

One fan wrote, ” Rest In Peace to a beautiful soul..”

Another cautioned, “We women got to love us first 100% before we love anyone else. Otherwise we will end up with the most beautiful children and a husband who cherishes us but we will still look in the mirror and not appreciate ourselves. And want more and more looking to being perfect while perfect has been right Infront of our eyes the whole time. I don’t know if I’m making sense English isn’t my first language but hope y’all feel me . Rest in peace”

We extend our deepest condolences to DC Young Fly, their children, and all those affected by this profound loss. May they find strength, solace, and support during this incredibly difficult time.
