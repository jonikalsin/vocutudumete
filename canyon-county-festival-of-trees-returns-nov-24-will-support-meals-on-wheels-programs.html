Canyon County Festival of Trees returns Nov. 24, will support Meals on Wheels programs

NAMPA, Idaho (CBS2) — The Canyon County Festival of Trees returns Friday, Nov. 24, at the Ford Idaho Center. Trees and vendors are open from noon to 6 p.m. on Friday and from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Saturday.

Breakfast with Santa is Saturday from 8:30 to 10:30 a.m. Bingo is also Saturday at 6:30 p.m.

The Canyon Country Festival of Trees will support the Nampa and Caldwell Meals on Wheels programs.

"Now more than ever, the support is needed to make sure no senior goes hungry. We have seen the need in the Caldwell area alone increase by 30 percent. In fact, we currently need at least ten additional volunteers to add more delivery routes to meet demands," said Julie Warwick, festival board member and Caldwell Meals on Wheels Coordinator.

Tickets are available at the door only. The prices are $5 for adults, $3 for Seniors (62+) and $3 for Children (12 and under).
