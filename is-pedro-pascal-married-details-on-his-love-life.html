Is Pedro Pascal Married? Details on his love life

Pedro Pascal is a Chilean-American actor best-known for playing Oberyn Martell in season 4 of Game of Thrones. Pascal delved into acting after his family emigrated from Chile to the United States. His early career mostly consisted of minor roles in shows such as Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Touched by an Angel, and Law and Order.

His breakout role in Game of Thrones earned him a Screen Actors Guild Award nomination for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama series. Pedro went on to land lead roles in Narcos and The Mandalorian. He will star in the 2021 film The Unbearable Weight of Massive Talent.

This piece will look at Pedro Pascal’s secretive dating life. Pedro Pascal is not married.

There were rumors that Pedro Pascal was the father of Lena Headey’s second child

Rumors about a relationship between Pedro and Lena started during his stint on Game of Thrones. The pair were regularly photographed together, and they occasionally posted each other on social media. At the time, Lena had just finalized her divorce from musician Peter Loughran.

The rumor gained traction after Lena got pregnant in late 2014. It was reported that Pedro was the father of Lena’s second child. However, it emerged that Lena was dating childhood friend Dan Cadan, and he was the father of Lena’s daughter Teddy.

It is unlikely that Lena and Pedro dated, but he did reveal that he had a crush on her. “Lena is one of my favorite persons,” Pedro wrote on Reddit. “And she is so funny and so smart, such a good person, a good friend and a good mother. I think everyone on that set and anyone who comes in contact with her develops an instant crush.”

Pedro reportedly dated Emergence actor Maria Dizzia in the early 1990s

Some tabloids report that Pedro and Maria Dizzia briefly dated in the early 1990s. Maria has appeared in Orange Is the New Black, Emergence, and The Undoing.

Reports have also linked Pedro with The Mentalist co-star Robin Tunney. The pair were photographed hanging out several times, but it is unclear whether they dated.
