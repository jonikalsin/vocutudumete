How many Super Bowls has Andy Reid won?

Andy Reid, the esteemed head coach of the Kansas City Chiefs, has achieved significant success in the NFL, particularly in the realm of Super Bowl victories. Here's a detailed exploration of his triumphant journey:

Super Bowl Triumphs

  • Super Bowl XXXI with the Green Bay Packers

    • Reid experienced his first taste of Super Bowl victory as an offensive assistant for the Green Bay Packers. The Packers emerged victorious against the New England Patriots.

  • Super Bowl LIV with the Kansas City Chiefs

    • In 2019-20, Reid led the Kansas City Chiefs to a remarkable Super Bowl victory against the San Francisco 49ers with a final score of 31-20.

  • Super Bowl LVII with the Kansas City Chiefs

    • Continuing his winning streak, Reid secured another Super Bowl triumph with the Kansas City Chiefs in 2022-23, defeating the Philadelphia Eagles with a score of 38-35.

    • Total Super Bowl Wins

    In summary, Andy Reid has won two Super Bowls as a head coach, both achieved with the Kansas City Chiefs. Additionally, he also secured a Super Bowl victory as an assistant coach with the Green Bay Packers.

    Andy Reid's Super Bowl Appearances

    Super Bowl Appearances

  • Super Bowl XXXIX with the Philadelphia Eagles

    • Reid led the Eagles to Super Bowl XXXIX but unfortunately fell to the Patriots.

  • Super Bowl LV with the Kansas City Chiefs

    • The Chiefs, under Reid's leadership, faced the Buccaneers in Super Bowl LV but were unable to secure a victory.

  • Super Bowl LVII with the Kansas City Chiefs

    • Reid's remarkable coaching led the Chiefs to triumph over the Eagles in Super Bowl LVII.

    • Total Super Bowl Appearances

    In total, Andy Reid has been to five Super Bowls in his illustrious coaching career. Notably, he has won two and lost one with the Chiefs, lost one with the Eagles, and was part of the coaching staff when the Green Bay Packers secured a Super Bowl victory.

    Andy Reid's Coaching Legacy and Achievements

    Coaching Career

    • Andy Reid is currently in his 25th season as a head coach in the NFL.
    • His remarkable track record includes a regular season record of 247-138-1 and a postseason record of 22-16, totaling 269 wins, which ranks him fifth in NFL history and second among active head coaches.

    Notable Achievements

    • Reid's coaching prowess includes three NFL Coach of the Year titles and continuous contributions to the NFL Competition Committee's Coaches Subcommittee.

    Andy Reid's legacy as an exceptional head coach in the NFL is underscored by his two Super Bowl victories with the Kansas City Chiefs, his extensive coaching experience, and his significant contributions to the sport of football. As a prominent figure in the NFL, Reid's impact on the game and his remarkable coaching achievements continue to inspire both players and coaches alike.
