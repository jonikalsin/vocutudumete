Eva Marcille is an actress, fashion model, and television personality from the United States. Moreover, Eva Marcille began her modeling career by winning the third season of America’s Next Top Model.

Eva Marcille Pigford was born on 30th October 1984, in Los Angeles, California, in the United States of America. As of now, this model is 39 years old and she is a citizen of America.

Moreover, Eva has a zodiac sign of Scorpio and she is a Christian by religion. In addition, she is the daughter of Evan Pigford (father) and Michelle Pigford (mother).

He has three brothers whose names are Evan Pigford Jr., Malcolm Pigford, and Andre Pigford.

Talking about her academic career, she studied at d Raymond Avenue Elementary School, Marina Del Ray Middle School, and Washington Preparatory High School.

Later, she attended Clark Atlanta University in Atlanta, after graduating from high school. She left the school, however, shortly after winning America’s Next Top Model’s third season at the age of 19.

Career and Professional Life

Talking about her professional career, Eva Marcille won America’s Next Top Model’s third season, defeating fellow contestants Yaya DaCosta, Amanda Swafford, and Ann Markley.

She received a modeling contract with Ford Models, a Cover Girl makeup bond, and a feast in Elle magazine.

This success boosted her modeling career, and she quickly found herself on the covers of Women’s Health and Fitness, Cosmopolitan, IONA, King, and Essence magazines. CoverGirl, Samsung, Red by Marc Ecko, Jewel magazine, In trace paper magazine, King magazine, WeTheUrban Magazine, IONA, Unbridled magazine, Star Magazine, Elle, Elle Girl, Applebottoms, Lerner Catalog, Avon Campaign 4, and Rolling Out have all featured her.

Eva’s runway shows have included Marc Bouwer Fall 2005, Elle Girl presents Dare to Be You: Wal-Mart Meets America’s Next Top Model 2005, Gharani Strok Fall 2005, Deborah Lindquist Spring 2006, Naqada Spring 2006, the 8th Annual ‘Models of Perfection’ Show 2006, and L.A. Fashion Week’s Monarchy Collection Fall 2007.

She decided to part ways with her managers, Benny Medina and Tyra Banks, on November 15, 2006.

She formally dropped “Pigford” from her name after leaving Banks and Medina for new management, now going as Eva Marcille (her middle name).

Further, she has indicated that she did this to avoid using her modeling popularity to go into acting and instead to earn her acting assignments.

Now, she is represented by L.A. Models. She has appeared on the CW’s America’s Next Top Model: Exposed. She names one of CoverGirl’s Top Models in Action.

Other works

She has appeared as a guest star on various UPN and The CW Network shows.

Moreover, she has appeared as herself in two episodes of Kevin Hill, one episode of Smallville, one episode of Everybody Hates Chris, and one episode of The Game.

She made an appearance in Tyler Perry’s House of Payne. Moreover, she hosted BET J’s reality show ‘My Model Looks Better Than Your Model. She also hosted ‘Rip the Runway on BET.

She features in various music videos, including Angie Stone’s “Baby” (with Betty Wright), 50 Cent’s “I Get Money,” and Jamie Foxx’s “DJ Play a Love Song.”

In 2005, she featured in an episode of the MTV improv show Nick Cannon Presents Wild ‘N Out’s first season. In December 2007, she appeared in an episode of Smallville.

In 2008, she joined the cast of The Young and the Restless as Tyra Hamilton, a young mother. Her role, which was supposed to be temporary, was made permanent after a few months.

She made an appearance in a June 2009 edition of BET’s prank show Played by Fame, in which players have nightmare dates with celebrities.

In 2010, she presented the first season of the Oxygen reality show Hair Battle Spectacular. In August 2013, she starred in French rapper Rohff’s video clip “J’accélère (I accelerate).”

On August 5, 2015, she was cast in the lead role of Tara in the TV show Born Again Virgin. She appeared in the reality series About the Business in February 2016.

Moreover, she competed in the first season of VH1’s Scared Famous in August 2017, which was broadcast on October 23, 2017.

She appears as a friend of Nene Leakes on Season 10 of The Real Housewives of Atlanta. She was promoted to a full-time cast member in 2018 for Season 11. In addition, she left the show after two seasons in 2020.
