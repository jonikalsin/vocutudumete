What to Know About Odd Eye Circles 2024 Volume Up Tour in the US and Mexico

Odd Eye Circle’s 2024 Volume Up Tour US and Mexico

Set to take place from January 12 to February 3, Odd Eye Circle, a South Korean girl group, will be embarking on their ‘Volume Up’ tour in 2024. This tour will cover multiple cities in the mainland United States and Mexico, in support of their latest EP released on July 12, 2023. After facing a hiatus due to contractual issues with their former label, Blockberry Entertainment, the group, formerly known as Loona, resolved the disputes and signed an exclusive contract with Modhaus.

Their tour will include stops in various cities, such as New York, Orlando, Atlanta, Houston, Tempe, Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Mexico City, offering a memorable experience for the fans in North America.

Odd Eye Circle Band

Odd Eye Circle is a South Korean girl group known for their unique blend of K-pop, dream-pop, and alternative R&B. It was formed as the second sub-unit of the larger girl group Loona as part of the “Girl of the Month” pre-debut project. Comprising three members – Kim Lip, JinSoul, and Choerry – Odd Eye Circle officially debuted on September 21, 2017, with their EP titled “Mix & Match.”

The trio gained recognition not only for their musical style but also for their legal dispute with their former label, Blockberry Creative, which they won in January 2023. Following the resolution, they signed exclusive contracts with Modhaus and resumed their activities, releasing their EP “Version Up” in July 2023.
