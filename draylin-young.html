Overall Meaning

The song "New" by Draylin Young is a Christian worship song that is a declaration of faith and reliance on God. The first verse speaks of the singer's faith and love being set on God and how His blood and grace have taken away their shame. The chorus is a declaration of being made new by Jesus and is repeated at the end of the song. The second verse speaks of how the singer puts their trust in God and declares that choosing His way, truth, and life is everything. The bridge of the song declares the singer's freedom in Christ and how they have been changed and given new life.


The song speaks about the joy and peace that comes with finding renewed hope and faith in God. It is essentially a reminder that no matter how far someone may have strayed, there is always an opportunity to start anew and follow the right path. Draylin's strong voice adds to the powerful message conveyed in the song.
