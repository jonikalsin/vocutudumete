KYLA PRATT AND DAUGHTERS ATTEND THE LITTLE MERMAID PREMIERE

Kyla Pratt, along with her daughters Lyric and Liyah Kilpatrick, attended the World Premiere of Disney’s “The Little Mermaid” on May 08, 2023, in Hollywood, California.

While the family looked cute and casual, some critics on Twitter expressed disappointment in their attire, assuming that they would be dressed in stunning gowns like other celebrities at the event.

“Kyla Pratt attended #TheLittleMermaid debut in my grocery store outfit,” one commenter said.

However, fans were quick to defend Pratt and her daughters, emphasizing that they were simply going for a simple and casual look, akin to going to the movies. One Twitter user shared a picture of the family, saying,  “People making fun of Kyla Pratt’s outfit at the #TheLittleMermaid premiere..yet she was at the premiere and y’all were at home.”
